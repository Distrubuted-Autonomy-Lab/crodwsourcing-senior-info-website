Project goals

Crowdsourcing Senior Info connects families and professionals with reliable local services. The team is working toward these outcomes:

  1. Public recommendations — simple, accessible submission of resource suggestions.
  2. Moderation — staff review (approve, merge, reject) with traceability.
  3. Similarity search — lexical and embedding-based tools to reduce duplicate entries.
  4. Data & deployment — PostgreSQL / PostGIS storage and Docker-based environments for demos.
  5. Security & documentation — hardening guidance and runbooks for sponsors and developers.

Emphasis areas

  • Caregiver support (e.g. dementia-related needs)
  • Rural and underserved communities
  • Data quality through moderation and deduplication
  • Programs spanning health, legal, housing, nutrition, and social support
